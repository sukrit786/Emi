Emicon TEAM (Teach, Engage, Aim and Mature) is about the “People” side of change management. Even the well-designed system can fail if the users are unable or unwilling to perform their roles in the new system. Our solutions are scalable and highly flexible to suit the diverse project sizes, corporate cultures and budgets.

Emicon TEAM delivers high quality, cost-effective and creative solutions to meet the training requirements for Oracle’s E-Business Suite and be-spoke solutions delivered along with it. We offer Oracle functional and technical training designed to fit into the lifecycle of your implementation or upgrade project. From executive courses on setting project expectations to workshops for project teams, to end-user, job-focused courses, Emicon TEAM empowers your team with the skills they need to make the most of your Oracle Application investments.

Emicon TEAM comprises experts and management professionals from top notch consulting firms and IT Advisory companies to bring onto the table the required value and expertise.
